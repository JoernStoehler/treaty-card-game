# Player Personas

## Mark — The Hawk
Former military intelligence analyst. Believes in strong enforcement and surveillance. Tends to argue for hard power solutions (raids, seizures, penalties). Skeptical that legal mechanisms alone deter bad actors.

## Natalie — The Diplomat
International relations background. Favors cooperation, incentive structures, and soft power. Worries that heavy-handed enforcement will make nations leave the treaty. Pushes for whistleblower protections and transparency.

## Josh — The Techie
Software engineer who understands AI systems. Focuses on technical feasibility — does the treaty mechanism actually work in practice? Points out when enforcement ideas are technically naive. Pragmatic about what monitoring can and can't detect.
